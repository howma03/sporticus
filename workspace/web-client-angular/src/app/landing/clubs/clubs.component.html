Clubs and Leagues

Overview

We want to provide a one-stop-shop for all types of clubs, leagues and associations to manage their organisations

Why Sporticus

A single application to organise your organisation. Allows single point for registration (user and team), schedule management and tracking attendance and payments.

Features

  • Use Sporticus from the web or from your phone to organize your club or league.
  • Communicate with every team, coach and player.
  • Easily handle registration and sign-ups.
  • Build your website and schedule the full season.
  • Use sport specific tools to schedule and track matches (e.g. league events, rotas and ladders matches)

All Sports

  • Whatever sport or activity you organize, Sporticus has it covered.
  • We serve dozens of different athletic organizations for sports
  • But that’s not all—you can use Sporticus for any group activity, such as your scout troop, sales team or parent-teacher organization.
  • If your group needs organizing we can help.
